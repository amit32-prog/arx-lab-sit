Principal Investigator

Lab Director

I am an assistant professor in civil engineering with a strong momentum toward an academic career built through interdisciplinary research, scholarly publishing, and grant development. My research aims to reimagine automation in construction by integrating robotics, artificial intelligence, physiological sensing, and immersive simulation.

I have contributed to a diverse portfolio of research projects — developing risk assessment frameworks for wearable robots, prototyping low-cost robotic systems for hazard detection, creating real-time physiological monitoring pipelines using wearable sensing and AI, and designing immersive virtual environments to support robotics education and workforce training.

I plan to build a research program that advances scalable, intelligent, and human-centered automation for construction — making infrastructure delivery more efficient, safer, and more equitable for workers worldwide.
