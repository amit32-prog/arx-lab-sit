Future directions

Five frontiers with room to grow into dedicated subpages later.

The original site listed future directions as a sequence of research frontiers. This redesign keeps that idea but presents each one as a modular timeline block that can later become its own detail page. fileciteturn2file1
01

Adaptive robots for collaborative autonomy

Robotic systems that adjust behavior in real time based on task progress, uncertainty, and human intent within active construction operations.
02

Low cost platforms for hazard detection

Affordable drones, legged systems, and compact robotic tools that broaden access to intelligent site monitoring and safety support.
03

Wearable intelligence for worker state estimation

Multimodal sensing pipelines that move from isolated experiments toward robust, near real time interpretation in realistic environments.
04

Immersive testbeds for design and validation

Virtual and hybrid environments that let the lab test risky or difficult scenarios before field deployment while preserving behavioral realism.
05

AI systems for future workforce training

Educational platforms that connect robotics, analytics, and visualization so future engineers can understand intelligent construction systems through direct interaction.
