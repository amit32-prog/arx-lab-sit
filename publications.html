Ongoing Research

Active Projects

Construction Robotics · Risk Assessment
Wearable Robot Risk Frameworks for Construction
Developing comprehensive risk assessment methodologies for exoskeletal and wearable robotic devices used by construction workers — addressing biomechanical load redistribution, human-machine interaction risk, and site-level deployment protocols.
Active · 2024–2025
Low-Cost Robotics · Hazard Detection
Affordable Robotic Platforms for Site Safety
Prototyping and field-testing low-cost robotic systems — including ground-based and aerial platforms — capable of autonomous hazard detection, site mapping, and safety compliance monitoring on active construction projects.
Active · 2024–2025
Physiological Sensing · AI
Real-Time Worker Health Monitoring Pipeline
Building end-to-end pipelines that stream physiological data from wearable sensors through edge-computing AI models to deliver real-time fatigue, stress, and thermal risk assessments for construction workers in the field.
Active · 2023–2025
Immersive XR · Engineering Education
VR-Based Robotics Education Platform
Designing and evaluating immersive virtual reality environments for teaching robotics, automation, and construction technology to undergraduate and graduate students — integrating scenario-based learning with performance assessment.
