§ I — Mission
"We build the systems that build the future — autonomously, safely, and at scale."

The ARX Lab sits at the frontier of civil engineering and intelligent systems. We integrate robotics, AI, physiological sensing, and immersive simulation to transform how infrastructure is conceived, constructed, and maintained.

From wearable robots on job sites to generative AI in the classroom, our work spans the full arc of human-centered construction automation — always asking: how do we make the built environment safer, smarter, and more sustainable?

§ II — Core Research
Current Work

Three Core Thrusts

I
Robots in Construction
Integrating autonomous systems into real-world construction. Human-robot collaboration, adaptive task planning, and field-ready low-cost platforms for unstructured, safety-critical environments.
II
Workplace Safety & Health
Real-time physiological monitoring using wearable AI to detect fatigue, stress, and risk exposure — enabling proactive safety interventions before incidents occur.
III
Immersive Education
Modernizing engineering education through XR environments and intelligent platforms — making complex robotics tactile, visual, and deeply engaging for the future workforce.
